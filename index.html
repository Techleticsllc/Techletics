QUESTIONS

Frequently asked

Does it feel different from a normal ball?

No. The sensor core is potted into the bladder during manufacturing, so weight, balance, and grip stay within regulation tolerances. Most players can't tell the difference in a blind test.

Which sports is Techletics built for?

The Gen 2 ball ships in football, basketball, and soccer builds. Firmware and app metrics are tuned per sport, so a spiral spin reading and a shot arc reading are both accurate out of the box.

How long does the battery last?

Around 18 hours of active tracking on a full charge. The ball sleeps automatically when it's still, so it won't drain in your gym bag.
